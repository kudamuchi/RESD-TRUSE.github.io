Who we are

 Renewable energy sustainable development Trust [RESD TRUST] is a non profit organisation whose mandate is to achievesustainable development through universal access to affordable clean energy and climate adaption and mitigation using renewable energy Technology. The organisation carries out community development projects.We also develop and implement solutions byconducting research into green innovative ideas that address the challenge of climate change andsustainable development in communities to build a better world and creating global impact.
